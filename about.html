About

Nelson Microgreens is a family business rooted in Carlisle Pennsylvania. We started out growing microgreens after reading about them online. The article claimed they were a superfood packed with nutrients and that they tasted better than their adult counterparts. Like most we were skeptical at first but as a family that loves gardening we were excited to try growing and eating something new. One taste and we were HOOKED! The flavors were out of this world! A little bit of microgreens on salads, soups, sandwitches, burgers, tacos and prettymuch anything else turned it into the star of the meal. We started selling microgreens in the beginning of 2022 and microgreens have been an absolute hit with everyone who tries them.
